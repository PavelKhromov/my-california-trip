Trip Information

Accomodation

Hilton Hotels were always an option, but we really enjoyed staying with AirBnb. Cozy cabin in Flagstaff, little appartment in the middle of San Diego, gorgeous Bay view house in San Francisco and magical, unforgettable hidden cabin in Yosemite Park.

Transportation

If you planing your trip and want to be secure about your transportation, you better check Car rental companies review before you give them your business. We were lucky enough to chose Enterprise Rent-A-Car.

National Parks

We would love to tell you how beautiful National Parks are but you better go and see it yourself. Little trick - buy the annual pass and definitely safe if you planing to see at least 3 parks. You can easylly spend two weeks in Yosemite National Park.

Food

In most cases, we cooked food ourselves, because in houses that we rented through AirBnb there were always excellent kitchens. If we stayed in hotels, our best assistant was the Yelp.
